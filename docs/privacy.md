# Privacy Policy

At Turbolong, privacy is a fundamental right. We are committed to transparency and data minimization.

## Information We Collect

We do **not** collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII), IP addresses, or use any third-party tracking cookies. 

We use a privacy-preserving analytics tool (Plausible) to understand general usage trends (e.g., page views, wallet connections, and deposits). This helps us improve the user experience without tracking individuals across the web.

## Do Not Track

We respect the "Do Not Track" (DNT) browser setting. If you have DNT enabled, our analytics scripts will not load, and your visit will not be recorded in our aggregated statistics.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
